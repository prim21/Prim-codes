Emergency Information

System Issues:

Contact shelter administrator immediately if system shows offline status or feeding errors occur.

Maintenance:

Weekly cleaning schedule recommended. Check food levels daily and refill as needed.

Power Backup:

System has 4-hour battery backup. Manual feeding required during extended power outages.
