About Us

Tails of Freedom Animal Haven

Tails of Freedom Animal Haven is a dedicated animal shelter that rescues and rehabilitates stray, abandoned, and neglected dogs and cats. Driven by compassion and a strong sense of responsibility, the shelter provides these animals with a safe environment where they receive proper care, medical attention, and the love they deserve.

Guided by the mission to Rescue, ReHab, and ReHome, Tails of Freedom works tirelessly to help animals recover from trauma and find forever homes. Beyond sheltering, the organization also raises awareness about responsible pet ownership and encourages the community to support humane treatment for all animals.
